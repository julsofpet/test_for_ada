Abstract

This study examines the geolinguistic properties of online communities, focusing on the correlation between subreddit nomenclature and semantic output. Utilizing a dataset of 858,488 posts spanning 67,180 unique subreddits, we analyze hyperlink behaviors to map global interactions.
