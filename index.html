Abstract

This study examines the geolinguistic properties of online communities, focusing on the correlation between subreddit nomenclature and semantic output. Utilizing a dataset of 858,488 posts spanning 67,180 unique subreddits, we analyze hyperlink behaviors to map global interactions.

Our journey is about looking through this "Orange Lens" to decode the vast, chaotic digital landscape of global communities.

We begin our odyssey equipped with data on over 858,488 posts spanning 67,180 unique subreddits.

Our tools include core variables like SOURCE_SUBREDDIT and TIMESTAMP, alongside rich text properties encapsulated in the 86-element POST_PROPERTIES vector (VADER sentiment & LIWC linguistic features).
